The Internet – Packets, Routing and Reliability

  • Back in the 1970’s there was no standard method for networks to communicate.
  • Vent Cerf Bob Khan invented the internet working protocol, this laid the ground for the internet.
  • Internet is the network of the network.
  • Protocol is a well-known set of rules and standards used to communicate between machine without trouble.
  • All different devices on the internet have unique address. An address on the internet is just a number, that is unique to each device on the network.
  • One of the most important protocols used in internet communication. It is called IP Address.
  • Visiting a website is just your computer asking another computer for access to this information.
  • Each set of data is represented in bits. The traditional IP addresses are 32 bits long.
  • The earlier numbers identify the country/network and region/network. Then the subnetworks then finally the address of the specific device.
  • This version is called IPv4 – Designed in 1973 and used in the 1980s.
  • Due to the internet being so popular they are making a new IP called IPv6. Creating 340 Undecillion unique addresses.
  • Most user do not see or care for Internet addresses. The Domain Name System, DNS, creates websites with corresponding addresses.
  • DNS servers are connected in a distributed hierarchy, so they can connect to millions of different addresses. For example, “. org, com, Edu”.
